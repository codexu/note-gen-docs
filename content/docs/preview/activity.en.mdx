Activity helps you review your usage rhythm. Events remain in the timeline even if the original note or record is later deleted.

## Open activity

- On desktop, open the activity panel for the heatmap and weekly total.
- On mobile, open Activity from the Me page.
- Select a day in the heatmap to view its timeline.

## Event types

| Type | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| Record | Creating or saving text, image, link, voice, and other records |
| Writing | Creating or editing Markdown files |
| Chat | Interacting with Chat or Agent |

The heatmap counts events; it is not a measure of words, time worked, or completed tasks. Activity is stored locally and may be included when records and settings sync is enabled.
