### Preset Themes

NoteGen provides 12 carefully designed preset themes, including both light and dark themes to meet different scenarios and preferences.

#### Light Themes

- **Default** - Classic minimalist white theme
- **Ocean** - Fresh blue tones for a peaceful working atmosphere
- **Forest** - Natural green tones, suitable for long reading and writing sessions
- **Sunset** - Warm red tones creating a comfortable writing environment
- **Lavender** - Elegant purple tones, perfect for creative work
- **Coral Warm** - Warm orange tones, full of vitality
- **Slate Gray** - Professional medium gray tones, business style

#### Dark Themes

- **Midnight** - Deep dark blue-purple, perfect for night work
- **Deep Sea** - Calm deep blue, eye-friendly and comfortable
- **Dark Forest** - Mysterious dark green, unique nighttime experience
- **Dark Violet** - Elegant dark purple, noble and elegant
- **Dark Gold** - Luxurious dark gold, showcasing quality

### Custom Theme Colors

In addition to preset themes, NoteGen supports custom theme colors, allowing you to create your exclusive personal style.

#### Color Configuration

Custom themes support setting the following colors:

**Light Theme Colors**
- Background - Main page background
- Foreground - Primary text color
- Card - Card and panel backgrounds
- Primary - Primary accent color for buttons and links
- Secondary - Auxiliary accent color
- Third - Additional accent color
- Muted - Background color for secondary content
- Accent - Accent color for highlighted content
- Border - Border and divider colors

**Dark Theme Colors**
- Supports the same color configuration as light theme
- Can set color schemes for light and dark themes separately

#### Share & Import

You can export your custom theme color configuration to share with other users, or import theme configurations shared by others.

#### Usage Recommendations

1. **Contrast**: Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background for readability
2. **Consistency**: Maintain overall color scheme coordination
3. **Scene Adaptation**: Choose appropriate themes based on usage scenarios, such as dark theme for nighttime
4. **Eye Care**: Use soft tones for long writing sessions, avoid overly harsh colors

### Mode Switching

- Supports manual light/dark theme switching
- Supports automatic switching following system settings
- Maintains current editing content and settings when switching
